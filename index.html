Your Preferences and Control Over Your Data

Choosing Your Preferences

You can access and adjust your account settings, including privacy and notification preferences, by logging into your MYCDRP account in our mobile apps or at www.mycdrp.com.

Opting Out of Marketing

You may opt out of marketing emails or SMS/MMS messages at any time by using the unsubscribe link in our communications or adjusting your settings. Please allow up to 30 days for opt-out requests to be processed.

Note: You will continue to receive service-related communications (e.g., account notifications, subscription updates, billing notices).

Notifications

  • Email notifications: Manage in your account settings.
  • Push notifications: Manage in your device's settings or within the app.

Advertising Preferences

We may work with third-party advertising or analytics partners. You can control personalized advertising in your device settings:

  • Android: Enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization" in your phone settings.
  • iOS: Disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track" in your phone settings.

Data We Collect

Depending on how you use MYCDRP, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email, username, password.
  • Payment Information: Payment details are securely processed by Stripe. We do not store your full credit card information on our servers.
  • Usage Data: App activity, interactions, content you upload.
  • Device Information: IP address, device ID, operating system, app version.
  • Location Data (if enabled): Used to improve features such as [insert specific feature].

We use this information to operate MYCDRP, provide services, process payments, and improve user experience.

Third-Party Services and SDKs

To operate MYCDRP and provide core features, we rely on trusted third-party providers:

  • Stripe – for secure payment processing and subscriptions.
  • Firebase (Google LLC) – for push notifications, crash reporting, authentication, and in-app analytics.
  • Google Analytics – to understand how users interact with the app and improve features and performance.

These services may collect information such as device identifiers, IP addresses, and usage patterns. Data is processed in accordance with their privacy policies:

  • Stripe: https://stripe.com/privacy
  • Firebase: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Analytics: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Controlling Your Data

We provide tools to let you:

  • Access, review, and update your personal data.
  • Correct inaccuracies in your data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Request export/portability of your data.
  • Restrict or object to processing of your data.

To make a privacy request, log into your account or contact us at privacy@sharezo.com.

Data Retention

We store your personal data as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may request deletion of your account at any time.

Children's Privacy

MYCDRP is not directed to children under 13. You must be at least 13 years old (or the minimum age of consent in your country) to use our services. Users under 18 may need parental or guardian consent to subscribe or create content. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Security

We follow industry standards to protect your personal data, including encryption, secure storage, and access controls. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

International Data Transfers

If you access MYCDRP outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the U.S. We rely on legal safeguards, including contractual protections, to ensure your data remains protected.

Your Rights (CCPA, GDPR, and other laws)

California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

You have the right to know what information we collect, request deletion, and opt out of certain sharing practices. We do not sell your personal information.

EU/UK Residents (GDPR)

You may exercise rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection.

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@sharezo.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated before they take effect. Continued use of MYCDRP means you accept the revised policy.
